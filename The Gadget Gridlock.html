The Gadget Gridlock
Marketing Myopia and the Mycelial Bypass
Stratum Level: Stratum V (5 to 10 Years) - Unified Systems
The explosive surge of smartwatches, EVs, and digital networks has collided with a rigid, decaying grid, prompting utility monopolies to lobby for heavy-handed consumption limits on daily life. In response, angry neighborhoods are utilizing rapid solar and battery advances to bypass these central chokeholds through decentralized, peer-to-peer "mycelial networks" of energy sharing. This grassroots defection forces legacy energy giants to cure their "marketing myopia" and transform into cooperative network enablers, or face complete extinction.
